MABHE Engineering was founded by Mandla Mkhwanazi in 2015 and it is based in the Gauteng Province of South Africa only a short drive from the OR Tambo Airport. MABHE is 100% Black owned and managed. The Company was created to allow the founder together with the Company’s associates to offer a range of professional services using their skills acquired through extensive training and work experience that has been gained in several roles.

The main business activity of the Company is the sourcing and supply of maintenance replacement equipment focusing mainly on fluid transport applications. The equipment supplied includes valves, piping, pumps and associated equipment and components. MABHE services include understanding the client’s requirements, agreeing on the technical specifications and sourcing the right equipment from reputable manufacturers. MABHE also has a Process Engineering division, which offers process engineering design services for capital projects and takes on process improvement assignments for existing operations.

During its Start-up phase MABHE will offer installation services by sub-contractors who wherever possible shall be local to its projects. MABHE also offers general project management services especially assisting small contractors to execute projects for big clients. That will include facilities management and maintenance type contracts.

MABHE Engineering is in partnership with Hytec Engineering. Hytec Engineering, established in 1972, combines state-of-the-art hydraulic cylinder manufacturing with extensive distribution and technical support to offer comprehensive cylinder solutions to the industrial and mobile markets. Hytec Engineering also offers holistic cylinder repair and testing, with a cylinder exchange offering and a world-leading test bench.
