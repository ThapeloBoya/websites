INDUSTRIAL EQUIPMENT

Mabhe focus has been on the manufacturing and refurbishment of selected steel components of which include automotive, rail, earthmoving, mining, agricultural and lifting equipment.

We now able to procure or rent out know fork lifts brands at preferantiial rates simple because of bulk orders made almost daily for all our customers nationwide.

With our in house logistics services division that offers consolidated loads for several commodities, such helps us improves our turn around time as we have enough orders to form a load almost daily with our distribution partners.

With one call we get you forklift at a good price or rental rate from any of the known brands on our bulk buying account and at negotiated payment terms with us.

For existing customers this service can be accessed on the same account already in place for steel components manufacturing, refurbishment or logistics with our mobility division.
