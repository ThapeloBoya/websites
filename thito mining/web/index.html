About
Thito Mining

Thito Mining is a woman owned mining company with shareholding partnership and presence in South Africa, Botswana and Zambia. Thito Mining has a specialized technical team that has an ability to identify and optimise value from undervalued assets. Our key product offering include Mining Investments, Mining Projects and Mining Solutions.

VISION

Building a top notch women owned mining company that makes sustainable impact in the Southern Africa market.

MISSION

To contribute to the growth and development of the economies we are in by resolving challenges such as high unemployment, reducing the inequality disparity, gender inequality and supporting SME growth and development through our employees, suppliers and subcontractors.

THITO MINING

Thito Investment

Our investment strategy is focused on sub-Saharan Africa with reliable mining regulations and title laws. We strategically seek relatively de-risked brownfield projects with valuable resources. Thito Mining Investments is a catalyst that unlocks value from asset with minerals with a robust market which require technical expertise or lack funding. These projects through our investment initiatives have a potential to be world class deposits positioned in the lowest quartile.

Thito Projects

Our key resource interests are in platinum group metals (PGMs), chrome, base metals (manganese) copper, energy (coal, uranium, oil and gas) and industrial minerals. We focus on de-risked Brownfields operations. All of our projects are on a long-term basis, with optimization of assets being the key goal. We invest in resource operations where we identify that our team can add value, by jointly and/or exclusively managing the operations.

Thito Solutions

Providing innovative mining solutions that contribute to the creation of a sustainable and greener future. Provision of off-grid solutions to our energy saving systems including functional and sustainable full “Turn-Key” solutions from Design, Manufacture, Installation through to Commissioning.
