ABOUT US

The TTE Group of companies has been serving South Africa with a wide range of road travel and transportation services. Established by Mandla Steven Mkhwanazi. The group now "consist" of 4 divisions all in the transport industry supplying unique services to its clients

TTE was formed in 2009 with its first operation in KZN, Durban and soon after opened a secodn branch in Richards Bay

Towards the end of 2009 TTE also got involved in shuttle service in collaboration with Vukanathi Transport Services. The joint venture of the two companies provides shuttle service to corporate companies namely, Chemserve Systems, Pla askem and Bob Martin all in the Chloorkop area.

In September 2010 TTE has expanded to Panel Beating & Car Service business operation in Durban along Sydney Road. The whole idea of having the combination of car rental and panel beating shop being a one stop for car insurance companies whereby in one call they can have a rented courtesy vehicle for their client while waiting for the vehicle that is being repaired. This combination is intended to put TTE on the competitive edge as insurances engage with two different companies to put together this package for their clients.

In the near future TTE will be seen nationally as a group of company providing these inter-related services ,shuttle, car rental and panel beating shop .The latter in house service being the assurance operation for keeping the fleet in good shape and complete a one stop shop for the insurance companies of which are TTE target market.
