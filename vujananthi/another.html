Beneficiaries

1. Learner-ships

Selected candidates get skills they require and working experience seamlessly from start to finish of the training program. This enables individuals to grow in their industries of choice and get career guidance at no cost to them.

2. Training Service Providers

As per the training needs analysis all candidates attend all the classes and catch up on missed classes. Candidates intending to pull out before the end of the program get proper advises and be made aware of the consequences before making their final decisions. We ensure that due payments are made on time and service provided is per agreed training plan.

3. Employers

Companies pay skills development levy (SDL) to the respective SETAs hence normally qualify for the grants for training programs for number of reasons .Meaning that over and above their core business 3 end up with the obligation of ensuring that the training program runs well from start to finish with the assistance of third parties like training service providers. It is advisable to every employer to take as many as possible candidates as permanent employees after completing their training program successfully as such input always help companies improve productivity and profitability ,become competitive and sustainable.

4. SETAs

With the SETAs we ensure that all contracts run well by closing all the gaps that are normally there and when not closed on time end up resulting in court cases, waste of time and major disruption of the skills development program. Our services add value to both discretionary grants and flagship programs at no cost to the SETA in question unless requesting for special service from our company still available at cost effective rates.
